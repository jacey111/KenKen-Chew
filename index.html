INTRODUCTION


KenKen, also known as Chew Jun Chuen, is a dedicated beauty professional with over nine years of experience in sales, customer service, and makeup artistry. A graduate of DIVA PRODUCTION Makeup Academy, KenKen holds a professional makeup certification and has gained valuable experience through prestigious sponsorships. His work includes collaborations on Astro video recordings, photoshoots, and backstage roles during Malaysia Fashion Week.

Currently, KenKen serves as a Senior Beauty Advisor and Artistry Team Member at Sephora Malaysia, where his responsibilities extend beyond sales to supporting the training department. His key tasks include conducting onboarding training for new joiners, enhancing their product knowledge, and helping them develop selling skills. He provides mentorship in general product knowledge, makeup techniques, and artistry skills to ensure they excel in their roles.

In collaboration with the marketing team, KenKen contributes to the planning and execution of events, including managing post-event reporting. Additionally, he organizes small-scale weekend activations to introduce customers to new products and drive engagement. His efforts consistently enhance team performance and elevate customer experiences.

With expertise in professional makeup application, skincare advising, content creation, and video editing, KenKen is known for his problem-solving skills, approachable demeanor, and adaptability. He consistently exceeds sales targets and contributes creatively to enhance customer experiences and team performance.
